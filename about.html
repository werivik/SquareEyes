About Us

Welcome to Square Eyes, the ultimate movie website for film lovers and supporters of small filmmakers! At Square Eyes, we are passionate about showcasing the talent of up-and-coming directors and providing a platform for their incredible stories to be seen and appreciated.

Our Cause

Our mission is to connect these filmmakers with audiences who crave unique and diverse cinematic experiences. We believe that every film has the power to make a difference, and -that's why we actively support these artists by providing a space where they can share their work and connect with the viewers. Through our subscriptions and buying individual movies features, you can contribute directly to the filmmakers you love, and help them bring their visions to life.

Background

Square Eyes was founded by a group of film enthusiasts who understand the challenges faced by independent filmmakers. With our background in the industry, - we are committed to promoting creativity, diversity, and innovation in cinema.We are dedicated to curating a collection of thought-provoking, entertaining, and inspiring films that you won't find anywhere else. Join us on this exciting journey as we celebrate the art of storytelling and support the next generation of filmmakers. Get ready to discover amazing movies, connect with talented directors, and be a part of the Square Eyes community.
